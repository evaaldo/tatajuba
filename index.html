Nossa história

Mostly done as a daytrip from Jericoacoara, but could easily be a stopover to or from Camocim. It offers to the tourists different landscapes and stops. The most common stop is at Torta Lagoon, you can have an excelent fresh grilled fish lunch enjoying the views from the lagoon. The beach ending is also one of the second stop recently discovered by the kitesurfers, it has one or two restaurants - the area is very pretty when the sea tide is full so the water comes very close to the restaurants & bungalows for rental. There are few places still to overnight, however is amazing the peace and relax this place offers. Its highly suggested to stay one or two nights you come to enjoy Jericoacoara or Camocim for more than a week stay.
